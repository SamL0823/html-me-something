The True Story of Goldilocks and The Three Bears

A Happy Family

Once upon a time, there lived a happy, law-abiding family of bears. There was Papa Bear, Mama Bear, and Baby Bear. The Three Bears lived a peaceful life in the woods, minding their own business and without a care in the world.

A Yummy Treat

One day, while Mama Bear was busy cooking some yummy porridge in the kitchen, a young girl was walking through the woods nearby. She smelled the porridge and was drawn toward the bears home. Goldilocks peeked through the window to see Mama packing a picnic basket. When lunch was all packed, she poured three bowls of porridge and left them to cool.

Intruder Alert

As soon as the bear family had left the house for their picnic, this theiving little wench climbed in through a window and went straight for the bowls of porridge! First she tasted Papa's, and it was too hot. Then, she tasted Mama's, and it was too cold. Then she tasted Baby Bears porridge and it was just right. She ate that poor baby's whole damn bowl of porridge.

After Goldilocks had filled her belly with stolen porridge, she needed to sit down. First she sat in Papa's chair... too hard. Mama's chair... too soft. Now this dumbass has sat all over the furniture with her messy porridge dress and she decides to sit her ass in the baby's chair. She's a big ass kid who eats way too much porridge and so, of course, THE CHAIR BROKE!

Goldilocks gives absolutely no f*cks at all, and so what does she do? After she's eaten this family's food and trashed their house? She goes to their bedroom and proceeds to roll around in all the beds like a damn psycho before finally falling asleep in the baby's bed.

When the bears came home to find their home had been burgled they immediately called the authorities. Unfortunately, Goldilocks woke and ran from the bears' house and into the woods before police could arrived.

The Aftermath

Several years after her encounter with the Bear family, Goldilocks was apprehended by police on other charges. She is now serving a life sentence in maximum security prison.
